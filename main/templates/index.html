About


An application to help the indecisive decide.

Hello! Welcome to our website. The purpose of this application is to help get rid of your indecision reguarding a particular subject. For our MVP, we have particularly completed the restaurant decider.

This application started with discussion of what to make for our portfolio project at Holberton School. Pondering about we thought about a decision maker for various subjects and even joked about a decison maker for a final project, but ultimately decided to start our focus on food and restaurants.

Whats to come

With the scope of this application there is alot that can be done, and with our time constraints we had to limit our focus on completing a single decider as an MVP. We originally planned on adding foods and recipes and once we can we plan to turn focus on creating a recipe decider that allows for user created recipes and foods. If possible there are thoughts of other deciders that utilize more of a database and will be whatever interests us at the time or whatever we end up needing because of our own indecision.
