Restaurant Decider


The decider for where to go out and eat!

Hey there! All that is required to get started is your location, but you may optionally input the price and rating. Then select the amount of results you'd like to choose from and hit "Roll" for a random selection of what's in your area. Feel free to select "Feeling Lucky?" for a single result, you could always re-roll or test your luck again!

If it's hard to choose from the results amount, you may remove what you don't like and click "Narrow" to randomly remove some selections. Also, if you make an account then feel free to click the little pink button to add it to your favorites, and purple to remove it! For more information, you can expand the categories, hover over the stars to see the number of reviews, and click on the "Yelp" to see it on their website.

Thank you for using our application, have fun!

(Results made possible by using the Yelp API)
